About

Still figuring it out — on purpose.

I'm a third-year Computer Science student who treats every course, club, and side project as a chance to make something real instead of just reading about it. I started with static websites, got curious about why they broke, and haven't stopped poking at things since.

Right now my time is split between frontend engineering, a bit of UI design, and writing short breakdowns of what I learn — partly for other students, mostly so future-me remembers how I solved it the first time.
